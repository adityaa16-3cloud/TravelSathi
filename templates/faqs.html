Frequently Asked Questions

What is TravelSathi?
TravelSathi is a smart travel planning platform that helps users plan trips, estimate budgets, and explore destinations easily.

Yes, TravelSathi is completely free for trip planning and exploration.
Does TravelSathi handle bookings?
Currently, TravelSathi redirects users to trusted third-party booking platforms for flights and hotels.
Is TravelSathi a real travel company?
TravelSathi is a project-based platform built for learning and demonstration purposes.
